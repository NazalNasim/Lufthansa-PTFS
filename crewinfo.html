Meet Our Crew Roles

Discover the variety of teams that make Lufthansa operations possible.

Crew Categories

👨‍✈️ Pilots

Responsible for operating flights, ensuring safety and smooth journeys worldwide.

🧑‍💼 Cabin Crew

Provide world-class service onboard, ensuring comfort and safety of passengers.

🛠 Engineers

Maintain and inspect aircraft, ensuring every plane is fit for safe operations.

🛰 Dispatch

Plan flight paths, monitor weather, and coordinate with pilots for safe navigation.

🛫 Ground Staff

Assist with check-in, boarding, and baggage — making travel smooth on the ground.

📡 Air Traffic Control (ATC)

Guide flights safely in the sky and on runways through precise communication.
